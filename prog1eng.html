Program for families - Istanbul

OTravel is looking for your comfort and enjoyment. This program is suitable for exploring and discovering exotic places, historical places, cruises, coffee and shopping. The tour begins with the reception, the receipt of OTravel gifts, the discovery of the breakfast street in Bashktas, the second day, and the program ends with a visit to Mini Turk (Mini Turkey), the last day passing through several fascinating places of discovery in Istanbul

-->

First Day

Welcoming

Welcoming the visitors at Ataturk airport and taking them to a hotel in the city of Istanbul and getting O Travel’s gifts

Second Day

Breakfast in Besiktas

Departure from the hotel at 8a.m. Breakfast street in Besiktas, this street opens early in the morning and closes at 2p.m. It is a street dedicated exclusively for breakfast

Bolmabahce Palace – The palace of astonishment

Departure at 11 a.m. This palace was the main center of the Ottoman empire, in 1856 the center moved from Top Kapi palace to Dolmabahce palace and continued to be the main center until 1922

Ortakoy

Departure at 2p.m. Departure for lunch in Ortakoy. Ortakoy is the eye of Istanbul. It oversees the Bosphorus. It is located in the European side of the city. It overlooks the Asian side of the city as well as the first pending bridge also known as the bridge of 15 July’s martyrs.

Rumeli Hisari Fortress

Departure at 5 p.m. Departure to Rrumeli Hisari Fortress after lunch. The fortress overlooks the bosphorus. Muhammed Fatih Sultan built it in only 4 months and he started off from it to open Constantinople. Its height reaches the 82 meters. It is a piece of beauty and it was a miracle because of its architecture and the decade it was built in.

Third day

Princesses Islands

Going to Kabatas at 10.30a.m. to take the steamboat to the princesses islands. The islands got its name during the Byzantine rule, they used to exile the princesses in it.Under the Ottoman rule, the islands were used as resorts for the rich people of Istanbul.

Forth Day

Istiklal Street

Having breakfast in the hotel then going to the independence street and taksim field to attend the yearly festival there that remains until the end of November (period of high temperature in the street).

Saphir Tour

Going to Saphir tour (6th highest tour in the world) after lunch, for shopping and enjoying the view of Istanbul from above the tour (boarding is optional)

Kanyon Mall – Ozdilek Mall

Going to kanyon mall (it won the best mall design in 2006), it includes more than 160 brand, most of them are international brands, it covers 37000 square meters. Next to it Ozdilek mall (the visitor can choose between the two malls or visit both)

Fifth Day

Optional trips

Mashukiye and Sapanca

Optional trips to Mashukiye and Sapanca, one of the most visited places in Turkey. It is the most famous and demanded trip, it starts early in the morning so you can enjoy its beautiful lakes and stunning views there and get some time in the forest to enjoy the spring.

Oriental nights tour in the Bosphorus

(Optional – the price of the tour is 35$ including dinner)
The tour includes:
-Music
-Moulawiya shows
-Sword shows
-Competitions and prizes
- Magic games

Sixth Day

Sultan Eyup Mosque

Departure from the hotel at 10am., after breakfast Going to Sultan Eyup Mosque which includes the tomb of the companion Abu Ayyub al – Ansari. There are a lot of activities around the mosque. The lunch will be at 1pm.

Pierre Loti Café

Going on foot to Pierre Loti Café on a high hill and an overlook on the Bosphorus. The Café was named after the French Poet who used to always sit there. This Café is considered as the fifth best Café in Istanbul according to Tripadvisor.

Teleferic

Going down towards the sea with the teleferic and going in an amazing trip on small boats to Miniaturk

Miniaturk – Mini Turkey

It is a mini garden on the north eastern beach of Istanbul, it includes sculptures of all the famous monuments in Turkey as well as some monuments around the world such as (Qubbet Assakhra mosque) .. and other games, appropriate for both kids and adults.

Seventh Day

Free day and departure

A free morning and departure in the evening.
